About Me

My name is Ruslan Akimov. I am a full-time student at Griffith University, studying a double degree: Bachelor of Engineering (Honours) / Bachelor of Data Science. I am entering my second year; over the coming four years I aim to build lasting friendships, make meaningful connections, and gain knowledge that helps me secure work I am genuinely interested in.

I was born in Armidale, NSW, and moved to the Gold Coast at a young age, where I attended AB Paterson College. Mathematics has always drawn me in, which led me toward engineering. I added data science because technology is evolving quickly — it complements engineering and gives me a strong parallel skill set.

I enjoy exercise and learning new things. My hobbies include the gym and sports, DJing, and electric guitar. I value empathy and connection, which I believe will matter for networking and the job market.

After university, I want to work as a civil engineer on infrastructure, and grow into more senior roles over time. Below is a snapshot of lessons and projects from my studies so far — including work related to 3101ENG Structural Analysis.
